5 Steps to better presentations

Oliver Zeigermann / @DJCordhose

http://djcordhose.github.io/ai/2017_do_machines_have_souls.html 

Disclaimer: I am not great at giving presentations, but without these recommendations, I would suck even more

0. Be excited about what you talk about (if even you are bored, it will be hard to motivate people to listen and participate). Giving the talk must be important for you.
1. Prepare presentation (do you need to start demo? Does it work?)
2. Rehearse (do you have enough time? Do you feel a logical flow?)
3. Don't overload slides. Never rush, just because you talk faster does not mean people understand more. Rather the opposite.
