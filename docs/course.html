Activation Functions (or non-linearity) take a single numerical input and performs a certain fixed mathematical operation on it.

Step, switching from zero to one
original version simulating transition from passive to active

Sigmoid, compressing between 0 and 1
continuously differentiable version of step function

Hyperbolic Tangent, floating from -1 to 1
like Sigmoid, but can also be negative

Relu: Rectified Linear Unit, Negative Values are zeroed out
often used in deep networks

Softmax: Categories with likelyhoods


predictions = tf.keras.layers.Dense(num_categories, name='classifier', activation='softmax')
