  • Machine Learning can learn from existing data and generalize from it
  • Sometimes, it can be an alternative to explicit, classic business logic
  • Classic Machine Learning strategies are often enough
  • Decision Trees and Random Forests are powerful strategies provided by Scikit-learn
  • TensorFlow lets you create your own Neural Networks
